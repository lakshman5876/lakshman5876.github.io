The developer attaches great importance to protecting your privacy. Before using this android app, please read carefully the following statements.

1. Ads

There are no ads in the app.

2. Crash Reports

App does not track crash reports.

3. Analytics

App does not collect diagnostic data or app analytics.

4. App Permissions.

No permissions are sought. If you want to save your data to cloud you may need to go through gdrive/account authorisation procedure
