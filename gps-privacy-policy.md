The developer attaches great importance to protecting your privacy. Before using this android app, please read carefully the following statements.

### 1. Ads
The developer has integrated Google Admob service provider into this Android app for getting mobile ads in an effort to monetize this free app.
 When you are using this app, the service providers might collect data and require some inputs like cookies and unique device id to render relevant ads tailored to you.
 This information is directly collected by the service providers securely. 
 The developer won’t collect any of your personal information.
 Please check privacy policy of this service provider to learn more.

### 2. Crash Reports
Anonymous crash reports are collected and transmitted to Google Firebase Crashlytics.
 This service is only implemented to bring bug fixes to the app, and it won’t include any of your personal information.
 When you are using this service, the information is directly sent to the service providers securely.
 Since the data is anonymous and there is no link with user, no facility for deletion of this data is available.
 User consent will be sought prior to collecting this data.
 Please check privacy policy of this service provider to learn more.

### 3. Analytics
Diagnostic data including UI flow, loading time, app versions and other critical debug variables are collected and
transmitted securely to Google Firebase Analytics.
 This service is only implemented to help us improve the app performance, and it won’t include any of your personal information.
 When you are using this service, the information is directly sent to the service providers securely.
 Since the data is anonymous and there is no link with user, no facility for deletion of this data is available.
 User consent will be sought prior to collecting this data.
 Please check privacy policy of this service provider to learn more.

### 4. App Permissions.
Location permission. This app needs to display GPS signal, speed, accuracy and other location data as part of its functioning and this implies requirement of Location permission.
This data is not collected or transmitted from the device and is only used for display of information to the user.
The user will be prompted for approval at the appropriate point in the app.
